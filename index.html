How to use:
1. Print (If you have access to printer): Fill out the form and click "Generate & Print" to create a ready-to-print A4 letter or use Save as PDF option to download and print later.
2. Handwritten: If you don't have access to a printer, scroll down to the "Reference Format" to write the letter on an A4 paper.
⚠️ Privacy Note: This application runs entirely in your browser. It does not store your data in a database or send your information to any server.
